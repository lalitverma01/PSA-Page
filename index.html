FlowCentric Processware is a business process management (BPM) workflow platform that enables the creation of seamless digital, form-based applications. Fully integrated with Pronto Xi ERP Software, FlowCentric Processware allows organizations to quickly build, integrate, and evolve process-driven job tasks, enhancing functionality without adding unnecessary complexity to the existing IT architecture.
